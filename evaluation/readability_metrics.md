# Readability Evaluation Metrics

## Purpose
Evaluate how well patient explanations simplify medical information.

## Metrics

### 1. Flesch Reading Ease Score
- Target: 60-70
- Formula: Based on sentence and syllable counts

### 2. Grade Level
- Target: 8th grade or below

### 3. Medical Term Reduction
- Metric: Percentage of jargon replaced
- Target: Greater than 80%

### 4. Sentence Length
- Target: Average 15-20 words per sentence

## Quality Checklist
- Medical accuracy maintained
- Jargon reduced by more than 80%
- Reading level appropriate
- Clear action items included
